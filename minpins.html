Why Choose a Miniature Pinscher

We at the We Heart Min Pins Foundation believe that everyone deserve a mini pinscher in their lives! Choose a Miniature Pinscher for their lively, compact size, low-maintenance coat, intelligence, trainability, affection, and loyalty, making them delightful companions.

Miniature Pinscher Facts

Statistics Value
Average Height 10-12.5 Inches
Average Weight 8-12 Pounds
Life Expectancy 12-15 Years

Source: the American Kennel Club
